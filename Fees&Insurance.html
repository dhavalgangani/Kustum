Fees & Insurance

Insurance

Kustom Rehab is an out of network provider for all PPO insurance plans. At the end of every month, Kustom Rehab will provide you with an invoice (also known as a “superbill”), which you can submit to your insurance company for reimbursement. Typically, you pay the full fee upfront and receive a percentage of it back, with the amount varying from plan to plan.

In order to get an accurate idea of your out-of-network insurance benefits, here are some questions you can ask your insurance provider:

Call the number on your insurance card and ask:

  • What type of health plan it is?
  • What does the policy cover?
  • What the policy does not cover?
  • Does your health insurance policy cover routine tests?
  • How much does the plan cost?
  • How is the policy premium determined?
  • How much money will you have to pay for availing medical care?
  • Will you be able to avail of treatment from your preferred doctor?

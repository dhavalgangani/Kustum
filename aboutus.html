About Kustom Rehab

Led by Dr. Rehab Kustom, Kustom Psychology is a private practice offering high-quality psychotherapy to adults and adolescents in the Westwood area of Los Angeles. Dr. Kustom is passionate about helping individuals understand the relationships, experiences, and sometimes the unconscious patterns that have shaped their lives. Through compassionate and collaborative exploration, Dr. Kustom’s goal is to help his patients build increased self-awareness and form new understandings of their past and present so they can feel good about the next chapter of their life.

Dr. Kustom’s philosophy is grounded in psychodynamic theory, with an integration of cognitive behavioral and mindfulness-based stress reduction techniques that can provide urgent symptom alleviation. Dr. Kustom has found this dual-approach most helpful in successfully decreasing feelings of anxiety, stress, depression, and anger issues.

Dr. Kustom’s office hours are by appointment only.
